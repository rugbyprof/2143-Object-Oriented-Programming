type: feedback

Terry appreciates honest, opinionated answers — when asked "is X better than Y?" he wants a real answer with reasoning, not a diplomatic "both are fine." He confirmed this when asking about ArgVal vs ArgType naming.

**Why:** He's teaching students and needs defensible positions, not hedged non-answers.
**How to apply:** When there's a genuine preference or best practice, state it clearly and explain why. Caveats are fine but shouldn't bury the point.
